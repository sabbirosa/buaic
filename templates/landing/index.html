About Us

The BRAC University AI Club is a vibrant community of students passionate about artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our mission is to foster learning, innovation, and collaboration in AI-related fields among students at BRAC University.

Through workshops, seminars, projects, and competitions, we aim to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in AI and contribute meaningfully to the field. We provide a supportive environment where members can explore their interests, network with like-minded individuals, and make a positive impact on society through AI.

Join us on our journey to explore the endless possibilities of AI and make a difference in the world!
